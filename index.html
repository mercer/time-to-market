time to market

Definition

As a Product Owner, I want my feature in production as soon as possible, so I can improve my product and be one step ahead of competition.

Time to market is the length of time taken in product development process from product idea to the finished product. It is a critical component of time based competition.

Definition vary cross the market because of business processes and complexities.
